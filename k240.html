JD's K240 page

K240 is a real-time 4X game released on the Amiga platform in 1994. The player competes with aliens to establish mining colonies in an unexplored asteroid belt. K240 is a sort of sequel to Utopia, and had a sort of sequel in the form of a very similar PC game called Fragile Allegiance.
